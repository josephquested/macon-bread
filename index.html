HOME CONTACT

I love BREAD! I know that life can be difficult - my practice has taught how to face these difficulties and find not only the strength to bear them but also how to face fully all of life’s experiences, from the joyful to the mundane. I know the value of a consistent practice, and I am honoured to share this with you.

I found bread while living in San Francisco, and have been fortunate enough to study with incredibly kind and intelligent bakers. I bring a compassionate and encouraging presence to my bread, and aim to make my bread relevant and meaningful to everybody.
